DistinctConcurrentBag in Spargine is a thread-safe, unordered collection that ensures uniqueness of elements, preventing duplicates. It offers methods for adding, removing, checking, and enumerating items, making it ideal for concurrent programming in .NET. For blocking behavior with uniqueness, DistinctBlockingCollection is recommended.
