Most of what runs on your servers was never meant to be public: databases, dashboards, admin panels, internal APIs. Tailscale connects your machines into one private, encrypted network so they can talk to each other (and to you) while the public internet sees nothing. Here's what it is and how to wire up applications across multiple VPSs with zero open ports.
