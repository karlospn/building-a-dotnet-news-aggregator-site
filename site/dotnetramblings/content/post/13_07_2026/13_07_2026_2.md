Pinning GitHub Actions to a full-length commit SHA is one of the simplest ways to reduce supply chain risk in your workflows. It ensures that a workflow keeps using the exact code you reviewed, instead of silently following a moving tag or branch. Tags are convenient, but they are mutable.
