Amazon Q Business comes with rich API support to perform administrative tasks or to build an AI-assistant with customized user experience for your enterprise. With administrative APIs you can automate creating Q Business applications, set up data source connectors, build custom document enrichment, and configure guardrails. With conversation APIs, you can chat and manage conversations with Q Business AI assistant.
