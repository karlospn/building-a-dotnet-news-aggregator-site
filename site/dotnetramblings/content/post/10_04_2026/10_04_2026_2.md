Beginning with PowerShell 7.7-preview.1 (April 2026), the MSIX package will be the primary installation method for PowerShell on Windows. We will no longer ship the MSI installer package for new PowerShell releases. For existing releases, including PowerShell 7.6, we will continue to provide MSI pack
