Deciding where shared logic lives is the most critical moment in Vertical Slice Architecture adoption, as choosing incorrectly reintroduces the coupling the architecture aims to eliminate. This article proposes a three-tier approach to sharing code that balances technical infrastructure, domain concepts, and local feature logic.
